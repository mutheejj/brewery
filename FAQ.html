FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services does the brewery store management system offer?

Our brewery store management system provides inventory management, sales tracking, customer management, and reporting functionalities.

2. Is the brewery store management system compatible with mobile devices?

Yes, our system is designed to be responsive and works well on both desktop and mobile devices.

3. How do I add new products to the inventory?

To add new products, log in to your account, navigate to the inventory section, and click on the "Add Product" button. Fill in the required information such as product name, description, price, and quantity.

4. What are the delivery options available?

We offer standard delivery and express delivery options. Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days, while express delivery arrives within 1-2 business days.

5. Are there any discounts available for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders. Please contact our customer support team for more information on bulk order discounts.

6. Is there a minimum age requirement to purchase from your store?

Yes, you must be of legal drinking age in your country to purchase alcoholic beverages from our store. Please drink responsibly.

7. Are your products suitable for people with dietary restrictions or health conditions?

We offer a variety of products, including options suitable for individuals with dietary restrictions or health conditions. Please check the product descriptions and labels for detailed information.

8. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping rates and delivery times may vary depending on the destination. Please contact our customer support for more information.

9. Can I return or exchange products?

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase, provided that the products are unused and in their original condition. Please refer to our return policy for detailed instructions on how to initiate a return or exchange.

10. Are your products organic or sustainably sourced?

We strive to source our ingredients from sustainable and responsible suppliers. While not all products are certified organic, we prioritize quality and environmental sustainability in our sourcing practices.

11. How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via email at support@brewerystore.com or by phone at 1-800-123-4567. Our customer support representatives are available during business hours to assist you with any inquiries or concerns.
